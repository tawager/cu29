About the Artist

I started creating jewelry 13 years ago and found my voice when I began experimenting with copper.

My experiences include working in a bead shop, completing an apprenticeship, obtaining my Accredited Jewelry Professional certificate from GIA and serving as a jewelry consultant at an auction house.

I strive to make all of my pieces a wearable work of art. They are all one-of-a-kind and up to the standard of this perfectionist!

Custom orders are welcome.
